Fondue

Embark on a delightful journey with fondue – a communal and cozy dining experience that revolves around melted cheese or chocolate. Gather your favorite dippables and indulge in the rich, creamy goodness of this Swiss culinary tradition.

Ingredients

  • Gruyère cheese
  • Emmental cheese
  • White wine
  • Garlic
  • Cornstarch
  • Kirsch (cherry brandy)
  • French baguette, cubed (for cheese fondue)
  • Fresh fruits, marshmallows, and cake cubes (for chocolate fondue)

Steps

  1. Grate the Gruyère and Emmental cheeses.
  2. Rub the fondue pot with garlic, then discard the garlic.
  3. Pour white wine into the fondue pot and heat it until it simmers.
  4. Gradually add the grated cheeses, stirring continuously until melted and smooth.
  5. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with Kirsch and add it to the cheese mixture, stirring until thickened.
  6. Adjust the consistency with more wine or Kirsch if needed.
  7. Serve with cubes of French baguette for cheese fondue or with fresh fruits, marshmallows, and cake cubes for chocolate fondue.
  8. Dip, swirl, and savor the delightful experience of fondue with your favorite companions!
