Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spices (e.g., oregano, basil)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature for pizza baking.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, distributing them evenly across the pizza.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the toppings and sprinkle herbs and spices for extra flavor.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a moment, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
